WHO WE ARE?

Wesley is an amazing faith community of diverse people who are committed to making faithful followers of Jesus. We do this by Growing Faith, Building Relationships, and Serving the Community. All of us are on the same journey, trying to learn more about who God has made us to be. There are ministries here for everyone- children, youth, and adults.
